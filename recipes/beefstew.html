Kenyan Beefstew

This classic French beef stew is the ultimate cold weather comfort food.

After a few hours in the oven, the meat becomes
meltingly tender and enveloped in a rich wine sauce.

Just like with most African recipes, actually all recipes, there is no cast in stone way of making East African or Kenyan beef stew.

normally based on the type of vegetables used.

The base of the stew is tomatoes and the key is in using fresh vegetables, which will depend on the seasonality.

Ingredients

      Cutting board
      Sharp knife
      A medium-sized pot
      6 or 8 Quart Instant Pot
      Stewing beef
      Spices
      Pantry staples
      Vegetables

    Steps

      Use your chopping board to cut your beef into stewing chunks.
      Using your blender or hand mixer, process onion, the remaining salt, tomato, red bell pepper, and tomato puree.
      Under high heat, heat a medium-sized pot or pan then add the oil.
      Serve this Kenyan beef stew with some ranch cauliflower mash or your favorite side dish.
